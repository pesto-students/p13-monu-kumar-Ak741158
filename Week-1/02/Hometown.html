Kalaburagi, Karnataka

Location

Kalaburagi, formerly known as Gulbarga, is a city in the Indian state of Karnataka.
It is the administrative headquarters of the Kalaburagi district and is the largest city in the region of North Karnataka.
Kalaburagi is 568 km north of the state capital city of Bangalore.
